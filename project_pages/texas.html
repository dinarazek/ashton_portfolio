TEXAS


This series of photos I took during a short trip to South Padre Island, TX. Here I learned a lot about surfing culture. Nicolas Motta pictured in many of the photos is from Houston, TX but like many local surfers, heads to South Padre Island when he wants to surf. I accompanied him on his surfing expeditions which took place around 5am or right before the sunrise. He explained that early morning was when you can catch the best waves. I also followed him to local surf shops where he would ask island natives about the best surfing spots. I was only there three days which turned out to be the days when there were very small to no waves but I was able to get closer to him when in the water due to these conditions.
