HOME IN BETWEEN HOMES


This project focuses on my temporary home. The early 20s poses a lot of uncertainty. One has to leave home to start his or her own life and go through a period of self discovery. During this period one stays in a house in which one knows he or she won't be in long. I wanted to document this state by photographing my relationships with the others in the house and our relationships with the house.
