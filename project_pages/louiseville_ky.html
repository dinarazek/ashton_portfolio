LOUISEVILLE, KY


Growing up in a small city like Louisville, KY can become boring after one has done everything there is to do before the age of 13. Myself along with many other teens in Louisville turned towards exploring with friends. I discovered abandoned building exploring was a very popular pastime once I entered high school. The most well known being The Ice Factory, an abandoned country club, various empty pools, and the pictured agricultural plant. Louisville is a fast growing city but this rapid growth has caused the newer and "hipper' parts of the city to leave behind and forget the not as pretty parts. This means most of the abandoned areas are in areas of lower income and higher crime rates which poses the question of whether we should be worried about the safety of the teens trespassing in these areas.
